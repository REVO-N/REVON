Product

This corrective pen is specially designed to help users improve their handwriting comfortably and effectively. It combines ergonomic design, guided control, and modular features to support proper writing technique.

The pen has a weighted front section (60–65%) that helps balance the hand and reduces strain, making writing smoother and more controlled. This weight distribution also encourages correct pen pressure.

Its ergonomic tri-grip design supports the thumb, index finger, and middle finger, helping users maintain a proper grip position. This reduces hand fatigue and improves writing stability, especially during long writing sessions.

The pen features a guided angled tip with a semi-fixed angle that is adjustable between 40°–55°. This helps users naturally hold the pen at the correct angle for better letter formation and consistent strokes.

An evolving grip system is included for progressive training. The grip can be changed from corrective grip, to transitional grip, and finally to a normal grip as the user’s handwriting skills improve.

Additionally, the pen offers an optional stylus tip module, allowing it to be used on digital devices such as tablets. A compact storage module is provided to safely store extra components when not in use.

Overall, this corrective pen is ideal for students and beginners who want to develop neat, comfortable, and consistent handwriting.
